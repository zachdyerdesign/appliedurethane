AUTI is a leading manufacturer of high quality, and long lasting urethane rollers. We offer High Grip (high co-efficient of friction) rollers with a precision ground to drive your products.

- Drive Rollers
- Pull Rollers
- Spline Rollers

- Transfer Rollers
